FAQ

Common questions from creators.

Is H/MIX GALLERY royalty-free music?

The catalog is original music by Hirokazu Akiyama with free-use conditions and commercial license options. Always follow the license terms for your project type.

Can I use these tracks for YouTube monetization?

Yes, YouTube use is supported when you follow the terms and credit guidance. For productions that cannot credit, use a commercial license.

Can I use this BGM in games?

Yes. The library is especially strong for RPG, fantasy, Japanese, horror, battle, and story-based games.
